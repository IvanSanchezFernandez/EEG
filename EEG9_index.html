SUMMARY

After reviewing this post, you have solidified the different stages of the sleep-wake cycle and the differences between epileptiform discharges and elements that mimic epileptiform discharges but are not.
You also learned a new type of EEG artifact: sweat artifact. You will recognize it immediately the next time you see it. Sweat artifact appears as a very slow swaying of the EEG channels.

Currently, you are able to differentiate pretty well EEG segments with and without epileptiform activity. Keep learning! There are exciting new characteristics of EEG coming soon.
